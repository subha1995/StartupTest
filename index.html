Coding & App Development

From beginner block coding to Python, web development and real app creation — structured across 4 progressive levels.

Age 6–16 4 Levels Live Classes Portfolio Projects

Curriculum Roadmap

Level 1

Scratch & Logic Building

Visual programming, animations, interactive stories and games. Develops computational thinking and problem-solving — no typing required.

Level 2

Web Design — HTML / CSS / JavaScript

Build beautiful, responsive websites from scratch. Learn design principles and publish live projects online.

Level 3

Python & Game Development

Write real Python programs, build console games, work with data and APIs. Strong foundation in software engineering principles.

Level 4

App Development & Launch

Build cross-platform mobile apps, integrate APIs and launch them to real users. Portfolio-ready capstone projects.

Robotics & AI

Hands-on STEM learning with real robotics kits, sensors, Arduino boards, AI tools and automation projects.

Age 8–16 Kit Included Live Classes STEM Projects

Curriculum Roadmap

Level 1

STEM Foundations

Physics of motion, basic circuits, electronics and the engineering mindset. Build confidence through hands-on experiments.

Level 2

Arduino Projects

Program real Arduino boards to control LEDs, motors and displays. First taste of hardware-software integration.

Level 3

Sensor-Based Robotics

Build robots that react to light, motion and sound. Create autonomous bots that navigate obstacles independently.

Level 4

AI + IoT Innovation

Connect robots to the internet, train simple AI models and build smart systems — the frontier of student innovation.

What's in the Robotics Kit

Financial Literacy for Kids

Teaching money habits, budgeting, saving and entrepreneurship — skills schools almost never cover but matter forever.

Age 10–16 Project-Based Live Classes

Curriculum Roadmap

Level 1

Money Basics

Understanding currency, income, expenses and the concept of value exchange. Why money matters and how it flows.

Level 2

Saving & Budgeting

Build a real personal budget, track spending habits and set meaningful financial goals. The 50-30-20 rule, made simple.

Level 3

Investing Basics

Stocks, mutual funds, compound interest and how time works in your favour. Made engaging and age-appropriate.

Level 4

Entrepreneurship

Launch a mini-business, learn pricing, marketing and pitch to real customers. Students leave with a working business plan.

Student outcomes

💼 Mini business plans 📈 Investing simulations 💳 Personal budgets 🎤 Pitch presentations

BrightRoots was founded on one belief: every Indian child deserves the skills that will define the future — coding, technology and financial intelligence. We make these skills joyful, practical and accessible for every family, wherever they are.

We partner with schools, work with expert educators and continuously evolve our curriculum so that every student builds something real — not just learns theory.
